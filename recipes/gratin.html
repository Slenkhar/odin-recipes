Gratin Recipe

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • cauliflower
  • bechamel sauce
  • salt
  • nutritional yeast flakes

Process

  • cook the veggies for 10 minutes in boiling water
  • drain and add the veggies to a gratin dish
  • add bechamel sauce, stir around, add salt and yeast flakes

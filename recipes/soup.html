Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • squash
  • garlic
  • vegetable stock
  • oil

Process

  • peel and chop all veggies
  • put it all in a big saucepan and roast them lightly with oil
  • cover veggies with water, add some vegetable stock and let it simmer for a good while
  • add spices to taste
